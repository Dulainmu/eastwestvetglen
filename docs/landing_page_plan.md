# East West Vets – Glen Waverley: Landing Page Plan

## Project Goal
- Announce the new Glen Waverley clinic.
- Leverage East West Vets credibility.
- Build trust and generate enquiries.
- Prepare for online booking (VetFlow) integration later.

## Content & Layout Strategy

### 1. Hero Section
- **Headline:** East West Vets – Glen Waverley
- **Tagline:** Holistic Veterinary Care – Combining Western & Natural Medicine
- **Subtext:** A new branch of East West Vets, bringing integrative veterinary care to Glen Waverley.
- **CTAs:**
  - [Call Clinic] (Primary)
  - [Directions] (Secondary)
  - "Online booking coming soon" (Text/Small Button)

### 2. About Section
- **Core Message:** Partnership with East West Vets, experienced team, locally owned.
- **Copy:** "East West Vets Glen Waverley is a newly opening clinic, proudly part of the East West Veterinary Group. We combine modern Western veterinary medicine with natural and holistic therapies to support the long-term health and wellbeing of your pets."

### 3. Philosophy (Key Pillars)
- Western Veterinary Medicine
- Chinese Medicine & Acupuncture
- Natural & Holistic Therapies
- Emotional & Physical Wellbeing

### 4. Core Services (Launch Scope)
- General health checks
- Vaccinations & microchipping
- Acupuncture
- Chinese medicine & herbal therapy
- Behaviour consultations
- Senior & geriatric care
- Palliative & end-of-life care
- *Note:* "Additional specialised services will be introduced as the clinic expands."

### 5. Trust & Credibility
- "Backed by East West Vets Bentleigh"
- Mention years of experience and link to the main site.

### 6. Location & Status
- **Address:** Glen Waverley, VIC (Update with exact when available)
- **Map:** Google Maps Embed
- **Status:** "Opening Soon" / "Now Open"

### 7. Contact Info
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Simple Contact Form (Name, Email, Message)
- Note: "Online booking will be available soon. For now, please contact us directly to enquire or make an appointment."

### 8. Footer
- © East West Vets – Glen Waverley
- "Part of East West Veterinary Group"
- "Online booking powered by VetFlow — coming soon"

## Navigation Structure
- Home
- About
- Services
- Location
- Contact


## Exclusions (Phase 1)
- No online payments.
- No full service encyclopedia.
- No blog/case studies yet.

## Design Implementation
The visual design has been implemented following the "Premium Holistic" aesthetic.
- **Design System Details**: See [docs/design_system.md](./design_system.md)
- **Key Features**:
  - `Playfair Display` & `Outfit` typography.
  - Deep Emerald & Warm Gold color palette.
  - Glassmorphism & organic background shapes.
  - Mobile-first, responsive layout.
